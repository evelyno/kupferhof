About Kupferhof Rosental

We believe that every couple's wedding should be a reflection of their unique story and personality. Our approach is always collaborative, seeking to uncover your vision and bring it to life in our historic courtyard.

Located in the heart of the wine country, our venue combines rustic charm with modern elegance, providing a canvas that is stunning and emotionally resonant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions from clients regarding full planning, coordination, and elopement services.

How Do You Charge for Services?
Our pricing is based on the scope of services required. We offer flat-rate packages for elopements and custom quotes for full wedding planning based on guest count and complexity.
What Is Included In Wedding Planning Services?
Our full planning includes vendor sourcing, budget management, design conceptualization, timeline creation, and day-of coordination to ensure a seamless experience.
How Much Time Do You Need to Plan?
Ideally, we recommend starting 12 months in advance for full weddings. For elopements, we can often accommodate plans with as little as 3 months' notice.
How Much Experience Do You Have?
With over 16 years of expertise in the wedding planning industry, we've built a reputation for creating unforgettable moments. We are supportive from initial consultations to the final goodbye.

Your special day should be filled with joy, love, and unforgettable moments. Our goal is to make your wedding planning experience enjoyable.
